Swarm Robots Platform

A responsive platform to make your swarm project a success
designed by Undergraduates Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya.

Having a Robotics Research... You are at the right place !

You are provided with marvalous features to accurately and securely do your research with us.

Icon

Automated Camera Setup

Automated obstacle bots are monitored by an overhead camera setup. The setup is highly adjastable and movable.

Icon

Interactive obstacle Bots

Obstacle robot swarm is capable of moving every individual obstacle robot to their own destination with the consideration of collision avoidance.

Icon

Automated Arena

The swarm robots are positioned in an automated arena which is capable of handelling upto hundred robots.

Icon

Positionning System

Robots are possitioned with high accurate positionning algorithms and optimized collision free mechanisms.

Icon

High Secure Database

Your project informations are stored in a database with high security. No attaker can get your information when you are with us.

Icon

User Friendly Interface

Bots can be moved to the desired positions with a user friendly interface.You are provided with easy positionning of your robots.
